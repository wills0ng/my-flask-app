Falling in love with a Classic

Apr 26, 2023 by Will

<< Back

After months of being on a wait list, I finally received in the mail my Leica M6 2022 reissue! Having said that, I am no stranger to Leica. My first camera from Leica was a Q2, which has a 28mm fixed lens on a 47-megapixel sensor. I've taken it around the globe and had it for about 2 years. After understanding the limitations of having a fixed focal length, I decided to take the plunge and trade in my Q2. Most recently I've been shooting film with a Contax T3 (point-and-shoot) and took the opportunity to add an M6 for more control over my images...not to mention gaining the ability to change focal lengths.

"I am no stranger to Leica"

I've come to appreciate film due to its aesthetic and nostalgia of using disposible film when I was a kid. My concern was how I would transition to it and what medium I would use (35mm or 120mm)? Not to mention there were virtually no manufacturers left that make new film cameras except for Leica. Cost aside, the serviceability of a Leica film camera by the OEM was definitely a huge factor that played in my decision to move to Leica.
