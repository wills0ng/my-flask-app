Falling in love with a Classic

Apr 26, 2023 by Will

<< Back

After months of being on a wait list...I finally received in the mail the most antipated camera I've been lusting for the other day, the Leica M6 2022 reissue! (Courtesy of a local camera shop in Seattle I used to frequent) Having said that, I am no stranger to Leica. My first camera from Leica was a Q2, which has a 28mm fixed lens on a 47-megapixel sensor. I traveled around with it around the globe for roughly 2 years. After understanding the limitations of having a fixed focal length, I decided to take the plunge and trade in my Q2 for an M11 at the Washington D.C. Leica store late last year (2022).
