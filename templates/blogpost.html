Falling in love with a Classic

Apr 26, 2023 by Will

<< Back

After months of being on a wait list...I finally received in the mail the most antipated camera I've been lusting for the other day, the Leica M6 2022 reissue! (Courtesy of a local camera shop in Seattle I used to frequent) Having said that, I am no stranger to Leica. My first camera from Leica was a Q2, which has a 28mm fixed lens on a 47-megapixel sensor. I've taken it around the globe and had it for about 2 years. After understanding the limitations of having a fixed focal length, I decided to take the plunge and trade in my Q2. Most recently I've been shooting film with a Contax T3 (point-and-shoot) and took the opportunity to add an M6 for more control over my images...not to mention being able to change focal lengths.

"I am no stranger to Leica"

I've been slowly acquiring Leica glass...I am currently using the 35mm Summilux steel rim reissue, 28mm Elmarit, 21mm Super-Elmar, and 90mm Elmarit-m. I also have the Zeiss 35mm F2.8 and Zeiss 50mm F1.5 which are both excellent lenses.
